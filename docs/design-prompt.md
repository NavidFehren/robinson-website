# Lovable Design Prompt: Robinson Boat Tours Website

Create a website for "Robinson Boat Tours", a private boat tour company in Mali Lošinj, Croatia.

---

## Company Overview

**Robinson Boat Tours** is a family-owned boat tour company based in Mali Lošinj, a beautiful island destination in Croatia known for its dolphins, hidden bays, and idyllic island landscapes.

- **Location**: Mali Lošinj, Croatia (Adriatic Sea)
- **Boat capacity**: Maximum 12 guests
- **Pricing**: Approximately 500€ per day for the entire boat (works out to ~42€ per person at full capacity)
- **Unique concept**: Private, intimate boat tours - the opposite of crowded mass-tourism excursions
- **Languages**: The captain speaks German, English, and Croatian

---

## Website Requirements

### Languages
The website must be available in three languages:
- German (primary - many guests are from German-speaking countries)
- English
- Croatian

A language switcher should be easily accessible.

### Pages Structure
1. **Home** (Startseite / Homepage)
2. **Tours** (Touren / Angebote)
3. **About Us** (Über uns)
4. **Gallery** (Galerie / Impressionen)
5. **Contact / Book** (Kontakt / Buchen)

---

## Page Content Details

### 1. Homepage

**Hero Section:**
- Prominent slogan, such as: "Discover Lošinj like a Robinson – Private Boat Tours with Heart"
- Background should accommodate a stunning image or video of the boat, sea, and islands

**Key Selling Points (4 bullet points):**
1. Maximum 12 guests – private, intimate atmosphere, no mass tourism
2. Individual routes & flexible stops – your day, your way
3. Family-friendly & personal service – warm hospitality from a local family
4. Dolphins & hidden bays – discover places only accessible by boat

**Call-to-Action Buttons:**
- "View Tours" / "Touren ansehen"
- "Inquire Now" / "Jetzt anfragen"

**Optional Elements:**
- Customer testimonial quote
- "Did you know?" fact about Lošinj (e.g., "Around 200 dolphins live in the waters around Lošinj")

---

### 2. Tours Page

Present three tour packages, each with: name, description, duration, capacity, price indication, what's included, and route highlights.

**Tour 1: Full-Day Island Hopping (Susak & Ilovik)**
- Duration: Full day (approx. 10:00 – 17:00)
- Description: Explore the unique sandy beaches of Susak island and the charming flower island of Ilovik. Includes swimming stops in hidden bays, village exploration time, and optional lunch at a local konoba (tavern).
- Included: Captain/skipper, fuel, snorkeling equipment, welcome drink, water
- Route highlights: Departure from Mali Lošinj, swimming bay stop, Susak island (village walk), Ilovik island, return via scenic route
- Price indication: From 500€ for the entire boat (up to 12 persons)

**Tour 2: Sunset & Dolphins Tour**
- Duration: Half-day/evening (approx. 3 hours)
- Description: An evening cruise to dolphin hotspots around Lošinj, ending with drinks while watching the sunset over the Adriatic. Perfect for couples or a romantic group outing.
- Included: Captain/skipper, fuel, snorkeling equipment, sunset drinks
- Route highlights: Departure in late afternoon, dolphin watching areas, scenic coastline, sunset spot
- Price indication: From 300€ for the entire boat

**Tour 3: Design Your Day (Custom Tour)**
- Duration: Flexible (half-day or full-day)
- Description: Create your own adventure! Choose your destinations, timing, and activities. Ideal for special occasions like birthdays, proposals, or team outings. Want to visit a remote "Robinson beach" that no scheduled boat reaches? We make it happen.
- Included: Captain/skipper, fuel, snorkeling equipment, personalized route planning
- Examples: Snorkeling safari, hidden beach hopping, combined boat & hiking tour, fishing trip, private celebration
- Price indication: On request – we'll create a custom quote

**Additional Information for All Tours:**
- What to bring: Swimwear, towel, sunscreen, hat, camera
- Food options: Guests can bring their own picnic, or lunch can be arranged at island restaurants
- Suitable for: All ages – children and elderly guests are welcome

### 3. About Us Page

**The Story:**
Tell the story of Robinson Boat Tours as a family-run business. The captain was born and raised on Lošinj and has been sailing these waters for decades. The family's love for the island and the sea is what drives the business.

**The Captain:**
Include a section with a photo and short biography of the captain. Mention:
- Years of experience
- Local knowledge and expertise
- Languages spoken (German, English, Croatian)
- Personal connection to Lošinj

**Why "Robinson"?**
Explain the name: The boat is called "Robinson" because it takes guests to places so untouched and natural that they feel like a modern-day Robinson Crusoe – discovering hidden beaches, secluded bays, and islands that most tourists never see.

**Our Philosophy:**
- "We welcome you with open arms"
- Personal attention for every guest
- Flexibility and accommodation of individual wishes
- Sharing the secrets and stories of the islands

**Inquiry Form:**

- Preferred date(s)

Note: "We'll confirm availability within 24 hours"

**Direct Contact Options:**
- Email address
- Phone number

- Note that the captain speaks German, English, and Croatian

**Location:**
- Google Maps embed showing the meeting point at Mali Lošinj harbor
- Brief text describing where to meet (e.g., "We meet at the main harbor of Mali Lošinj, at pier X, next to...")

**FAQ Section:**

*What should we bring?*
Swimwear, towel, sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, camera. Snorkeling equipment is provided.

*Is food included?*
We provide water and a small welcome snack. For full-day tours, lunch can be arranged at an island restaurant, or you're welcome to bring your own picnic.

*Are children welcome?*
Absolutely! We love having families aboard. The captain ensures a safe and fun experience for guests of all ages.

*Can elderly guests join?*
Yes! Our captain assists with boarding and disembarking. The boat is comfortable for guests of all mobility levels.

*What happens if the weather is bad?*
Safety comes first. If conditions are unsafe, we'll contact you in advance to reschedule or offer alternatives.

*Can we bring our dog?*
Please ask us – in most cases, well-behaved dogs are welcome aboard.

## Target Audiences

The website should appeal to:

1. **Families with children** – looking for a safe, private, child-friendly experience where kids can explore freely
2. **Groups of friends (6-12 people)** – sharing costs makes it affordable; looking for a fun day of swimming, snorkeling, and sun without strangers
3. **Couples** – romantic sunset tours, private beach visits, special occasions like anniversaries or proposals
4. **Small companies / teams** – team outings, incentive trips, or small corporate events (up to 12 people)

---

## Key Messaging Themes

Throughout the website, emphasize:

1. **Private & Intimate** – "Your own boat for the day" – no crowded tour boats
2. **Flexible & Customizable** – "Your day, your way" – routes adapted to guest wishes
3. **Authentic Local Hospitality** – Family-run, captain knows every secret spot, warm personal service
4. **Adventure & Discovery** – "Feel like Robinson Crusoe" – hidden beaches, untouched nature
5. **Natural Beauty** – Dolphins, crystal-clear water, stunning islands, unforgettable sunsets

---

## Additional Notes

- The website should feel welcoming and personal, not corporate
- Emphasize that this is the opposite of mass tourism
- Trust signals: mention years of experience, local expertise, happy customer quotes
- Mobile-friendly is essential – many tourists browse on phones while on vacation
- Fast loading with optimized images
- Clear calls-to-action on every page
